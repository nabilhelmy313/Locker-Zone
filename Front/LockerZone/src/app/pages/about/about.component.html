About
Our project, which is to build an application for booking lockers, will display information about available lockers for students who want to rent a locker. The student can also specify the period for which he wants to reserve a locker. He may need to pay an amount to reserve the locker, and the student can also renew the reservation. Among the advantages of this site: The platform provides students with an easy way to reserve a place to store their important belongings during the daily university activities. The system provides an easy-to-use booking system that allows students to book a locker for their preferred duration, as well as an online payment gateway for paying the required duration. The ability to reserve a locker for a longer period of time with a subscription renewal, making it a flexible and specialized solution to meeting students' storage needs. Lockers is a reliable and convenient service that offers undergraduate students an easy and stress-free way to reserve a place to store their belongings while they are on campus. Through the lockers website, students can easily browse the reserve lockers on campus on their site to book a locker with ease.
